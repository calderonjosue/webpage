## Overview

Pipod is a Colombian company with **14 years in the market**, specialized in Apple product sales and technical service. I support the company's digital transformation and its B2C/B2B growth strategy.

## My Role and Key Contributions

### 1. Business Strategy
- Authoring of the master **Business Plan**
- Full SWOT analysis and commercial model definition
- Market research on Colombian Apple-focused competition
- Buyer persona definition and premium tech segment profiling

### 2. Digital Transformation
- Full development of the **e-commerce platform** using WordPress and WooCommerce
- Hosting, domain, and security setup
- UI/UX design of the entire online store
- Quality assurance of the e-commerce experience

### 3. Digital Marketing and Growth
- Design of the annual marketing plan
- Advanced **Meta Ads** strategy (campaigns, segmentation, creative planning)
- SEO, blog structure, content, and acquisition funnel
- Graphic creatives (Canva)
- Implementation of the **AARRR** model and definition of the **North Star Metric**

### 4. Project Management
- Project management using Kanban methodology
- Coordination via Trello and Miro
- Strategic meetings with leadership
- Cross-functional team collaboration

### 5. Training and Documentation
- Internal training on the use of AI for sales and operations in small businesses
- Creation of the corporate **Brandbook** (excluding logo)

## Technologies and Tools

- **E-commerce**: WordPress, WooCommerce
- **Marketing**: Meta Ads, SEO, Google Analytics
- **Design**: Canva, Figma
- **Project Management**: Trello, Miro, Kanban
- **Metrics**: AARRR Framework, North Star Metric

## Challenges and Learnings

Transforming a traditional 14-year-old business into a digital-first company required balancing legacy processes with modern e-commerce practices. I learned to apply QA principles not just to software, but to entire business processes, ensuring quality across marketing funnels, user experience, and operational workflows.

## Outcome

Pipod is undergoing a digital expansion phase, strengthening processes, structure, and scalability toward a stronger, more competitive business model. The project demonstrates my ability to:
- Lead digital transformation initiatives
- Apply QA thinking to business strategy
- Bridge technical and business perspectives
- Drive measurable growth through data-driven decisions
